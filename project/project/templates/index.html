I graduated from the University of Massachusetts, Dartmouth in 2000 with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Graphic Design & Letterform where I learned about all the important elements of design, including form, color, typography, layout, etc.

I started my professional design career as an in-house Graphic Designer at Sunderland Printing in Hyannis, MA. After that, I worked for over 5 years as a Junior-turned-Senior Visual Designer at Hotwire, an Advertising & Design Agency based in Providence, RI.

During this time, I was able to further develop my skills in print design as well as design for the ever-growing web. Since then, I have been increasingly interested in web design and front-end web development.

For more information about me, check out my professional LinkedIn Profile.
